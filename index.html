In the field at Boston Harbor Islands National Recreation Area (2015)

Hi there! My name is Shanasia Sylman and I currently work as an Outdoor Recreation Planner for the National Park Service (NPS). Essentially, I travel around the country assisting NPS park units with park management problems.

Working for the National Park Service is one of many interests, and I hope next to explore the worlds of geospatial analysis, data science and cartography. Federal park planning has been a serendipitous exploration of my core profession: urban planning. A blend of science, design and research focused on broader planning issues is my next destination.
